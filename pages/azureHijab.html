Abaya

The abaya "cloak" is a simple, loose over-garment, essentially a robe-like dress, that covers the whole body except the head, feet, and hands.

Jilbaab

Jilbāb or jilbaab refers to any long and loose-fit coat or outer garment..
French Jelbab.. roomy and comfortable 2 piece clothing

Khimaar

A long veil worn as a head cover.. part of hijab.
