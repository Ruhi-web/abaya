Abaya

Previous Next

These are our beautifully elegant butterfly, kimono sleeve abayas. They are wide, flowy and offer full coverage with a lovely drape. These abayas also have kimono style sleeves with snap buttons on the inside of the sleeve which fit around your wrists. The button is very convenient for when you need to make wudhu. The long frontal zip also offers convenience for breastfeeding mothers!

It is wide and has excellent coverage like a jilbaab and can be paired with our stunning diamond khimaars giving you full coverage and that modest yet prestigious look!
