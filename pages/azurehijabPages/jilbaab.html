Jilbaab

Previous Next

Our stunning two piece Jilbaabs are comfortable, simple and classy. They are made from premium, wool peach material and are great for both hot & cold days! They are loose fitting and provide great coverage, including cuffed wrists. Also, there are two additional straps inside which can be used for an optional pull up niqaab.

These Jilbaabs come with a matching skirt which have two convenient pockets on each side. The skirt is also equipped with an elastic waistband which makes it suitable for sisters of all sizes.

We have three sizes which means it can fit sisters of all different heights.
